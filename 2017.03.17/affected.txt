This bug affects SQLite versions 3.10.0 through 3.16.2 (inclusive).
Exercising the bug results in a segmentation fault.

This bug was reported to the SQLite3 development team, who implemented
a fix that was included in the 3.17.0 release.

The bug lasted just over 13 months (2016.01.06 - 2017.02.13) before it was
patched.
